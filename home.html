Count down the days with us:



September 10th 2025

Here is a timeline of our love story...

(Click on the smaller photos to enlarge)

September 12th 2021

Sofia's first message to Liam was "What are the names of your cats??". They got talking and the rest is history.

September 22nd 2021

Our first date at Mooboo Bubble Tea in Cardiff. Sofia made Liam walk around Cardiff and he frequently complains this was annoying. They settled on a bench in Alexandra Gardens to talk and Sofia breathed a sigh of relief when during their conversation, she found out Liam did not harbour any extreme right-wing views that would make dating a brown girl from Easton near impossible. She found him really interesting. They discussed tattoos, politics, and their family - not knowing they would one day become each others'.

October 22nd 2021

Liam asked Sofia to be his girlfriend and the first "I love yous" were messaged over Whatsapp whilst Sofia was drunk at a drag night with her friend Maddie. Liam promised not to say it too much but he lied. Every day has been filled with "I love you" since this moment.

December 25th 2021

Their first Christmas together. Liam had bought a star for Sofia. Sofia made him a memory box from the last few months together, including letters, songs, and poems Sofia had wrote for him.

January 4th 2022

Liam and Sofia spent Liam's birthday together at an Italian restaurant in Caerphilly. A surprise tart came out with a candle in it! The bus to Rhymney did not show up so Sofia booked a table last minute, saving the day.

March 13th to 18th 2022

Our first holiday was to Krakow where we stayed in a strange hotel but enjoyed the sights thoroughly. It's always a good week when you have McDonald's and play in a ballpit. They spent Sofia's birthday in Mr Pancake. Memories were made that day.

April 23rd 2022

Quickly after they had met, they moved in together. They became a family unit: 2 people and 2 cats.

October 22nd 2022

Their first anniversary.

December 25th 2022

Liam bought Sofia 2 guitars for Christmas, named Starling and Iblis. One of the most thoughtful things anyone has ever done for Sofia.

October 22nd 2023

Their second anniversary.

October 29th 2023

Their first pumpkin picking together with friends at Avon Valley Adventure Park.

October 31st 2023

Their first sober Halloween rave together.

February 29th 2024

Sofia had planned a romantic evening together. First, a meal at the Butcher's Arms which provided a quality meal for the hungry pair. After the meal, they set off to the Brecon Observatory. Unfortunately, they got lost and were awaiting a response from the activity coordinator. The activity coordinator returned saying the weather was too snowy for the activity to go ahead. However, Sofia saw a moment on the side of the road to talk about the Engagement Ring Asterism. The couple had been looking at engagement rings prior to this. Sofia had a speech written up. One where she had to include a some words about letting her ramble as Liam did in fact try to interrupt. He said yes in the car and they returned home for nosecco and berries, letting friends and family know about the news.

August 10th 2024

Sofia and Liam's engagement party. Much of their family and friends attended. It was a brilliant night full of memories.
